Tortang Talong

Description

Tortang Talong is a classic Filipino dish that is simple, healthy and easy to prepare. It has a nice texture and smokey flavour that can be served as a main or a side. This dish can be eaten for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 Asian eggplants
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon onion, minced
  • 1 teaspoon glarlic, minced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Boil or grill the eggplants until they are tender and the skin is charred and blistered in appearance.
  2. Let cool and peel the skin of the eggplant, retaining the crown and the stem.
  3. Gently flatten the body of the eggplant usin ga fork being careful not to break the eggplant, leaving it in one whole piece. Set aside.
  4. In a bowl, beat the eggs, add in all the seasoning.
  5. Heat the cooking oil in a pan over medium-high heat
  6. Dip each eggplant into the egg, one at a time.
  7. Bring the bowl near the pan, tilt the bowl and gently lower the eggplant into the pan.
  8. Fry the eggplant until it is golden brown, flip over and brown the other side.
  9. Remove the eggplant from the heat and let it cool on a paper towl.
  10. Repeat for remaining eggplants.
  11. Serve with your choice of rice, sauces and garnishes.
